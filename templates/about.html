About Me

This is what I do.

Hi, I'm Alliyan Shahid.

I'm a Python Developer with 7+ months of professional experience building full-stack web applications, REST APIs, and backend systems. I currently work at Developer Tag (PVT) LTD, where I contribute to projects like customer management systems, business applications, and digital platforms.

Before diving deep into development, I founded and scaled an e-commerce business (Shopy Worlds) to 300K+ PKR in monthly revenue — managing everything from product sourcing and digital marketing to customer relations. That experience taught me how to think beyond code: how to solve real problems, communicate clearly, and take ownership.

Alongside development, I also work as an Accounts Assistant, handling financial records and monthly reporting. It's a role that keeps my attention to detail sharp and reminds me that technology works best when it meets real-world needs.

I'll soon be moving to Sweden (August 2026) to pursue a Bachelor's in Computer Science at Kristianstad University, and I'm actively looking for part-time Student Worker opportunities where I can contribute, learn, and grow.
